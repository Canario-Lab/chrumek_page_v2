FAQ

Common questions before downloading Chrumek.

You know exactly what you are installing: Chrumek helps you follow through on real saving, but it does not connect your bank, make transfers, or require an account.

Do I need to connect my bank?

No. Chrumek does not connect to your bank. You move the money wherever you want: a separate account, an envelope, or a piggy bank.

Does Chrumek make the transfer for me?

No. The app helps you close the ritual: you log the amount, set it aside in real life, and then confirm in the app that the money is already on your side.

What if I log an amount but never set it aside?

Then you do not confirm the transfer. That is the point: charts, history, and goals should grow only after the money is truly set aside.

Is my data sent to a server?

The data you enter in the app stays locally on your device. You do not create an account and you do not hand your decision history over to some external dashboard.

How do Premium and the free trial work?

You can try the app before paying. Continued access to paid features is handled through the App Store, and any trial depends on the offer currently available to that user.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Chrumek supports CSV export, so you can take your confirmed savings and goals outside the app.
